Data Deletion

Last updated: July 9, 2026

Tunnelwave does not require a user account and does not provide account creation inside the game.

Local Game Data

Tunnelwave stores some game data locally on your device, such as coins, settings, owned themes, selected badges, daily challenge progress, and local Hall of Fame scores. You can delete this local data by uninstalling Tunnelwave or by clearing the app storage in your device settings.

Deletion Requests

To request deletion of data associated with Tunnelwave where deletion is available, email BattouGames at battougames@gmail.com.

Please include the app name, your request, and any relevant details that help identify the data you want deleted. Do not send payment card details or sensitive personal information.

Advertising And Analytics Data

Tunnelwave may use Google Analytics for Firebase and Google AdMob. Some analytics or advertising data may be controlled by Google privacy settings or advertising ID controls on your device. You can reset or delete your advertising ID from your Android privacy settings where supported by your device.

Google advertising privacy information is available at https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads.

Contact

For privacy, data deletion, support, or purchase questions, contact: battougames@gmail.com
