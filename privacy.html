Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Tunnelwave handles information. Tunnelwave is a lightweight arcade game by BattouGames.

Information Stored On Your Device

Tunnelwave may store gameplay progress and settings locally on your device, including coins, owned themes, options, lives, daily challenge progress, and local Hall of Fame names and scores. This local data is used to run the game and keep your preferences.

Advertising

Tunnelwave may show ads through AppLovin MAX and mediated advertising partners. These third parties may collect or process information as described in their own privacy policies and data sharing requirements.

AppLovin privacy information is available at https://legal.applovin.com/privacy/.

Purchases

Tunnelwave may offer in-app purchases. Purchases are processed by Google Play. Tunnelwave does not directly receive your payment card details.

Children

Tunnelwave is not directed to children. If this changes, this policy and store settings will be updated before release.

Safety Notice

Tunnelwave contains bright neon visuals, rapid motion, screen rotation, and flashing light effects. Stop playing immediately if you feel dizzy, nauseous, disoriented, notice vision changes, twitching, or any seizure-like symptoms. If you or your family has a history of epilepsy or seizures, consult a doctor before playing.

Contact

For privacy or support questions, contact: battougames@gmail.com

Changes

This policy may be updated as Tunnelwave develops, especially when production advertising, purchases, analytics, or store requirements are finalized.
