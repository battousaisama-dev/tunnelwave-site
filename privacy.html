Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Tunnelwave handles information. Tunnelwave is a lightweight arcade game by BattouGames.

Information Stored On Your Device

Tunnelwave may store gameplay progress and settings locally on your device, including coins, owned themes, selected badges, options, daily challenge progress, and local Hall of Fame names and scores. This local data is used to run the game and keep your preferences.

Analytics

Tunnelwave uses Google Analytics for Firebase to understand how the game is played and to improve balance, stability, usability, and future updates. Analytics events may include gameplay actions such as tutorial progress, run starts and finishes, score, zone reached, risk gates, pickups, selected themes or field skins, purchases completed through Google Play, and similar in-game events.

Google Analytics for Firebase may also collect app-instance identifiers, advertising identifiers, app lifecycle information, session information, in-app purchase events, and approximate location derived from masked IP address. Tunnelwave does not request precise GPS location.

Analytics data is transmitted using encryption in transit. This data is not used to let users create accounts in Tunnelwave.

Advertising

Tunnelwave may show rewarded ads or interstitial ads through Google AdMob / Google Mobile Ads SDK. Tunnelwave does not use banner ads. Advertising partners may collect or process information, including advertising identifiers, as described in their own privacy policies and data sharing requirements.

Google advertising privacy information is available at https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads.

If AppLovin MAX or other mediated advertising partners are enabled in a later update, this policy will be updated to describe those partners.

Purchases

Tunnelwave may offer in-app purchases for virtual coins or other in-game content. Purchases are processed by Google Play. Tunnelwave does not directly receive your payment card details.

Data Deletion Requests

If you have questions about privacy or want to request deletion of data associated with Tunnelwave where deletion is available, contact BattouGames at battougames@gmail.com. Some locally stored game data can also be removed by deleting the app or clearing the app's storage on your device.

Children

Tunnelwave is not directed to children. If this changes, this policy and store settings will be updated before release.

Safety Notice

Tunnelwave contains bright neon visuals, rapid motion, screen rotation, and flashing light effects. Stop playing immediately if you feel dizzy, nauseous, disoriented, notice vision changes, twitching, or any seizure-like symptoms. If you or your family has a history of epilepsy or seizures, consult a doctor before playing.

Contact

For privacy or support questions, contact: battougames@gmail.com

Changes

This policy may be updated as Tunnelwave develops, especially when advertising partners, purchases, analytics, or store requirements change.
