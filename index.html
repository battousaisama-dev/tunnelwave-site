About

Tunnelwave is a fast first-person arcade game built for short, replayable runs. Tap to rise, release to fall, and thread through the tunnel as speed and risk climb.

Optional 2X routes trigger hyperdrive and permanently raise the stakes for the rest of the run.

Safety

Tunnelwave contains bright neon visuals, rapid motion, screen rotation, and flashing light effects. Stop playing if you feel unwell, dizzy, disoriented, or notice any seizure-like symptoms.
